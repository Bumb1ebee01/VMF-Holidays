## 5. Earning Credit — The Referral Programme

### 5.1 How referrals work

You share your unique referral link or code with a friend. If that friend uses your link or code and later travels with VMF, both of you may earn credit as set out below. Rewards are earned on **completed travel — not on booking or enquiry alone.**

### 5.2 Attribution (who gets credited)

(a) A referred friend is attributed to the referrer whose link or code first brought them to VMF (**first-touch attribution**). The referral is recorded when the friend clicks a valid referral link, enters a valid code, or identifies their referrer at enquiry.

(b) A referral link records attribution for a limited period (currently **60 days** from first click). If a person is introduced by more than one member, VMF applies first-touch attribution.

(c) A person cannot be "referred" if they are already a VMF customer or an existing Club member, or if they have already enquired with VMF independently.

(d) VMF determines attribution using its own records. **VMF's determination of who referred whom, and whether a reward is payable, is final.**

### 5.3 Referred-friend welcome credit

Your referred friend receives a one-time **welcome credit of ₹1,000** when **all** of the following are met:

- they joined or enquired through your valid referral link or code;
- they complete their first VMF trip; and
- the booking value of that trip is **₹25,000 or more**.

The welcome credit is paid once per person, ever. A booking below ₹25,000 does not earn the welcome credit.

### 5.4 Referrer reward

As the referrer, you receive a reward when your referred friend **completes a qualifying trip**, provided:

- the booking value of that trip is **₹25,000 or more**; and
- the reward is approved by VMF under Section 5.5.

The reward amount depends on your tier at the time the reward is confirmed:

| Your tier    | Referrer reward |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Explorer     | ₹2,000          |
| Voyager      | ₹2,000          |
| Navigator    | ₹2,500          |
| Ambassador   | ₹3,000          |

A referrer reward is paid at most once per qualifying referred friend.

### 5.5 Reward approval and limits

(a) Rewards are earned **only on trips that are actually completed**. No reward is earned on an enquiry, a quotation, a cancelled trip, a no-show, or a refunded booking.

(b) VMF operates the Programme sustainably. VMF may, at its reasonable discretion, **decline, reduce, defer, or cap** any referrer reward — including where the reward is disproportionate to the value or margin of the underlying trip, where a booking is discounted or made at cost, or where VMF reasonably suspects abuse. The referred friend's welcome credit under Section 5.3 is not affected by a capped or declined referrer reward, provided its own conditions are met.

(c) Credit is confirmed only after VMF has verified the completed trip and its booking value. Amounts shown as "pending" or "expected" in your dashboard are indicative and are not guaranteed until confirmed.

(d) VMF may set, vary, or remove a lifetime limit on total referrer rewards per member at its discretion.
